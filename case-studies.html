Law Firm Research Automation

Seattle litigation firm cuts research time in half — in 30 days.

A 6-attorney Seattle litigation practice was spending 12–18 hours per case on legal research. Associates were pulling all-nighters before trial prep, and the managing partner was watching billable hours drain into administrative work.

Agent 425 conducted a 3-day workflow audit, identified the highest-ROI automation targets, and deployed an AI research agent integrated directly with their existing Westlaw subscription. The agent surfaces case law, flags conflicting precedents, and produces structured research briefs — reviewed by the associate, not written by them.

Within 30 days, average research time per case dropped from 14 hours to under 6. The managing partner estimated $65,000 in recovered billable time in the first quarter alone.

Home Services Scheduling & Dispatch

Bellevue HVAC company doubles job volume without adding office staff.

A 12-technician HVAC company in Bellevue was losing 20+ service calls per month to after-hours voicemail. Their single dispatcher was overwhelmed during peak season, and scheduling conflicts were costing them 2–3 missed jobs per week.

Agent 425 deployed a 24/7 AI voice agent that answers every call, qualifies the job, and books appointments directly into their ServiceTitan calendar. A second AI layer optimizes technician routing each morning based on location, skills, and job priority.

In the first 60 days, missed call revenue recovered by $38,000. Dispatcher overhead dropped 40%, and the owner expanded to a second service area without hiring additional office staff.

Law Firm Client Intake

Tacoma family law practice converts 3x more consultations with AI intake.

A solo family law practitioner in Tacoma was personally fielding every initial inquiry — often during client meetings or court. Potential clients who didn't get a callback within hours were calling the next firm. Conversion from inquiry to paid consultation was below 25%.

Agent 425 implemented an AI intake system that responds to every inquiry within 90 seconds, asks qualifying questions, assesses case fit, and schedules consultations directly into the attorney's calendar — even at midnight.

Consultation conversion rate climbed from 22% to 71%. The attorney reclaimed 8+ hours per week previously spent on intake calls, and monthly new client revenue increased by 44%.
